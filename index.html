About Our Program

At CodeLab After School Program, we believe every child deserves the opportunity to explore technology, develop computational thinking, and build skills for the digital future. Our comprehensive program combines coding education with creative activities, project-based learning, and character development.

We provide a structured yet flexible environment where children can learn programming fundamentals, work on exciting tech projects, participate in robotics and electronics activities, and develop important skills like logical thinking, creativity, and teamwork.

Our experienced instructors are passionate about technology education and dedicated to creating a positive, inclusive atmosphere where every child feels supported and inspired to innovate.
